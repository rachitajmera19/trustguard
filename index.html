2. Context & Situation

🎯 Setting

Urban India: Students, young adults & homemakers.

Heavy daily use of Instagram, WhatsApp & UPI.

Informal social commerce (no payment safety net).

⚡ Triggers

  • Fake bank/authority calls
  • Advance-payment Instagram scams
  • Phishing links & body-shaming comments
  • Fake profiles & "digital arrest" manipulations on elderly

🚫 Response Gap

Most victims deleted/ignored rather than reported — didn't know how to file a complaint.

Reporting felt too complex or futile — police rarely acted on small cases.

🧠 Awareness Gap

Schools teach theory, not practice. Cybercrime portal unknown to most.

Elderly & rural users most exposed; families constantly rely on younger members for every decision.

3. Evidence Base

📍 VB/IT Case - Telangana 2023-24

Women targeted via coordinated accounts. WhatsApp/Instagram morphed photos & threats to leak explicit content.

4 arrested within days, ul AIR.

📍 Rama Desai - Hyderabad 2024

Victim targeted by 4 coordinated accounts — vulgar abuse & false propaganda.

Cybercrimes Police - 2 arrested under IT Act.

📍 Aarav Khan - Hyderabad

16 yr old bullied with unverified comments on a birthday photo. Confused after post deleted. Needed counselling, never formally reported.

📊 UN Report 2020 - 50,000+ Children Surveyed
  • 60% say cyberbullying is rising.
  • 82% don't report (fear of stigma).
  • AI deepfakes creating new threat vectors.
  • Reputational harm spreads in seconds.
⚖️ Legal Framework (India)
  • IT Act: Sec 67 (obscene content)
  • Sec 67A: (explicit material)
  • IPC/BNS: Sec 499 (defamation), Sec 354D (cyberstalking)
  • BNS 2023 replaced IPC from July 2024.

4. Pain Points & Needs

  • ⚠️ No escalation path: Known red flags but next steps not clear.
  • ⚠️ Hidden features: Report button buried deep in settings, no in-context guidance.
  • ⚠️ Blind spot: Zero confirmation, ticket ID or status update after reporting.
  • ⚠️ No support: No helpline easily found; cybercrime portal unknown until much later.
  • ⚠️ Lack of knowledge: Doesn't know body-shaming qualifies as cyberbullying or is reportable.
  • ⚠️ Fear of reprisal: Fears reporting will escalate, not resolve the situation.
  • ⚠️ Self-censorship: Copes only through avoidance, self-censors to reduce risk.
  • ⚠️ Age exclusion: No simple, trusted digital safety guide suited for 45+ users.
  • ⚠️ Authority trust: No real-time striking alert from bank; scam call felt completely official.
  • ⚠️ Systemic failure: Filed FIR but money never returned.

5. Root Causes (5 Whys)

Core Problem: No trusted path to protection or recovery
L1 Main Branches: Reporting buried, no status, victims siloed, users excluded.
L2 Immediate Causes: Report friction, no ticket ID, no shared fraud signal.
L3 Design Decisions: Safety not in roadmap, vs reported content, no UX+ onboarding.
L4 Systemic Gaps: Audits shadow, FIR limits fraud recovery, FIR not linked to recovery.
L5 Root Incentives: Penalties rarely applied, no enforcement SLA, no cyber insurance.

6. How Might We...?

7 HMW questions - shortlisted from 100 ideas across 7 concepts

HMW 1: create a single API call that freezes funds across all linked banks within 60 seconds of a fraud report?
HMW 2: give every user a one-tap SOS from the lock screen that works even in panic or low literacy situations?
HMW 3: make digital fraud recovery status live and publicly visible so victims stop waiting and banks stay accountable?
HMW 4: warn users about local scam threads in their dialect before they are targeted?
HMW 5: make high value transactions safer for elderly & low-literacy users through voice and traffic light risk signals?
HMW 6: give every user a legal 60 second right to retract a payment they were coerced into making?
HMW 7: restructure fraud helplines like a KYC ... with playbooks, peer counsellors, and cross-bank case tracking?

7. Top Ideas

Top 3 Quick-Win Ideas - shortlisted from 100 clustered concepts

1. National Fraud Freeze API

High value ~40 Low effort

An NPCI mandated open API where a single authenticated call from the victim fans out a freeze instruction to every linked P2P simultaneously — with a 40-second SLA defined in RBI master direction.

2. One-tap SOS Lock-Screen Trigger

High value ~43 Broad reach

A one-tap hotkey logged accessible from the lock screen, Quick Settings, and a short code voice line... Backed by a Family Vault where 1-3 verified contacts are alerted.

3. Fraud-Event ID Replaces FIR Paperwork

High value ~40 Victim relief

A digitally auto-generated, shareable fraud-event ID issued at the moment of impact — removing the need to physically visit a police station and file an FIR manually. The ID is recognized by banks, the cybercrime portal, and telcos... Reduces victim burden from days to seconds.
