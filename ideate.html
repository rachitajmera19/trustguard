Phase 03 · Ideate

Ideation Workshop

A structured 6-3-5 Brainwriting session with 6 participants — generating 108 ideas in 30 minutes, clustered and scored into 7 actionable solution concepts.

The Workshop

6-3-5 Brainwriting Method

The 6-3-5 Brainwriting technique generates a large volume of ideas without groupthink. Six participants each write three ideas in five minutes — then pass their sheet to the next person, who builds on or branches from those ideas. Six rounds = 108 ideas total.

This approach was chosen over traditional brainstorming because it eliminates social pressure, prevents dominant voices from suppressing quieter ideas, and forces participants to build on each other's concepts rather than starting from scratch each round.

Step-by-Step Process
1
Prime with the HMW
The selected HMW question is shared with all participants. Five minutes of silent individual review before writing begins.
2
Round 1 — Original Ideas
Each person writes 3 completely original ideas on their sheet. No filtering, no self-censorship. 5 minutes.
3
Pass & Build
Sheets rotate to the next person. They read the existing ideas, then write 3 more — inspired by, building on, or departing from what they read.
4
Six Rounds Complete
Each sheet has passed through all 6 participants. 108 ideas captured in total — 30 minutes of focused ideation.
5
Affinity Clustering
Ideas are read aloud, placed on a wall, and clustered by theme. 7 distinct concept clusters emerge.
6
Score & Select
Each cluster scored on impact, feasibility, and novelty. Top concept identified for prototyping.
All Seven Concepts

108 ideas → 7 solution concepts

After clustering and scoring, 108 raw ideas became seven coherent, actionable concepts. Each concept addresses a distinct layer of the digital harm problem.

01
National Fraud Freeze RPC
A one-tap OS-level SOS mechanism that triggers a cross-bank transaction freeze and notifies the National Cybercrime Portal within 2 minutes. Accessible from the lock screen — no login required.
High Impact High Feasibility Novel
02
System-Level SOS & Family Vault
A trusted-contact network giving family members visibility into suspicious transactions and the ability to pause transfers — protecting vulnerable and elderly users through a guardianship model.
High Impact Medium Feasibility
03
Live Status Broker & SLA Scoreboard
A transparent public dashboard showing real-time status of fraud reports — replacing institutional silence with accountability. Banks and police are scored on response time publicly.
High Impact High Novel
04
Scam Weather Forecast Feed
Hyperlocal scam pattern intelligence surfaced proactively before users make high-risk transactions — like a weather alert for digital fraud hitting your area or demographic.
Medium Impact High Novel
05
Voice-First Pre-Confirm & Inclusive UX
Voice-guided transaction confirmation in regional dialects — designed for low-literacy and elderly users who are disproportionately targeted by phone-based scams but excluded by text-heavy UX.
High Impact Medium Feasibility
06
Right to Retract
A 60–120 second window after a UPI transfer is confirmed to cancel it — similar to email undo-send. Gives users a critical safety buffer in high-pressure manipulation situations.
High Impact Regulatory Challenge
07
SOC-Style Fraud Desk & Peer Counsellors
A human-backed Security Operations Centre combining trained fraud investigators with peer support counsellors — providing both technical triage (who to call, what to freeze) and emotional first aid to victims in the immediate aftermath of a fraud or harassment incident.
High Impact Resource-Intensive High Trust
